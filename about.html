Our Heritage & Mission

For over two decades, Falalu & Sons International has stood as a beacon of academic excellence and character development, nurturing the next generation of global leaders.

Our History

Established in 1998 by visionary educator Alhaji Falalu, our institution began as a humble primary school dedicated to quality education in Minna. Driven by a commitment to academic rigor and moral uprightness, the school rapidly expanded.

Today, Falalu & Sons International is a premier educational complex encompassing pre-primary, primary, and secondary education. We blend traditional values with modern pedagogical approaches, ensuring our students are equipped to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
